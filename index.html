I am a second-year PhD student in Mathematics at the University of Heidelberg. Before that, I completed my Master's in Mathematics at ETH Zurich and my Bachelor's in Physics at the University of Bologna.

My research is in symplectic geometry and hyperbolic dynamics. Specifically, I study Anosov flows and their relation to Liouville geometry. I also work on Lagrangian dynamics from a variational standpoint.

In parallel to the above line of research, I work on geometric machine learning and information geometry. I am currently interested in flow matching.
